Understanding Your Hair Type

Type 3: Curly Hair

From loose ringlets (3A) to tight corkscrews (3C), curly hair thrives on moisture and definition. Look for lightweight creams and gels that enhance your natural pattern without weighing it down.

Type 4: Coily Hair

From S-shaped coils (4A) to tight Z-pattern kinks (4C), coily hair is incredibly versatile and beautifully unique. It craves deep moisture, gentle handling, and rich butters and oils that seal in hydration.

Why Porosity Matters

Your hair's porosity (how well it absorbs and retains moisture) is just as important as your curl pattern. Low porosity hair needs lightweight products and heat to open cuticles. High porosity hair benefits from heavier creams and protein treatments to fill gaps in the hair shaft.
