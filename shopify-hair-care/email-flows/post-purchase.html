Get the Best Results

Here is how to make the most of your new products

1

Cleansing Day (Shampoos & Co-Washes)

  • Wet hair thoroughly with warm water to open cuticles
  • Apply a quarter-sized amount to your scalp, not lengths
  • Massage gently with fingertips in circular motions
  • Rinse completely -- leftover cleanser can cause buildup
  • Follow immediately with conditioner while hair is still wet
2

Deep Conditioning (Masques & Treatments)

  • Apply generously to clean, damp hair in sections
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or fingers to distribute evenly
  • Cover with a plastic cap and apply gentle heat for 20-30 minutes
  • For low porosity hair: heat is essential to open cuticles
  • For high porosity hair: rinse with cool water to seal cuticles
3

Styling (Creams, Gels & Oils)

  • Apply to soaking wet hair for best curl definition
  • Use the praying hands method to smooth product through
  • Follow with shingling or finger-coiling for extra definition
  • Seal with oil on top for high porosity hair
  • Air dry or diffuse on low heat -- never rub with a towel
4

Scalp Care (Serums & Oils)

  • Part hair in sections and apply directly to the scalp
  • Massage in circular motions for 3-5 minutes to boost circulation
  • Use 2-3 times per week for best results
  • Apply at night and cover with a satin bonnet for overnight treatment
  • Consistency is key -- results typically show in 4-6 weeks

Pro Tips from Our Crown Experts

♦

Sleep on satin. Swap your cotton pillowcase for satin or silk. It reduces friction, prevents breakage, and keeps your styles lasting longer.

♦

Hydrate from within. Drinking enough water is the most underrated hair care tip. Your curls are thirsty from the inside out.

♦

Detangle gently. Always detangle from ends to roots with a wide-tooth comb or fingers, on wet, conditioned hair. Never force through knots.

♦

Protective style wisely. Give your hair breaks between protective styles. Keep them in no longer than 6-8 weeks and keep your scalp moisturized.
