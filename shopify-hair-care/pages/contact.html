Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

How do I know which products are right for my hair type?

We recommend starting with our Hair Type Quiz. It takes about two minutes and considers your curl pattern, porosity, density, and styling goals to recommend a personalized CrownKinetics regimen. You can also browse products by hair type in our collections, or reach out to us directly for one-on-one guidance.

Are CrownKinetics products safe for color-treated hair?

Yes. All CrownKinetics products are sulfate-free and formulated with gentle, pH-balanced cleansers that will not strip color. Our products actually help preserve vibrancy by sealing the cuticle with natural oils and butters. We recommend our Monarch Seal Leave-In Conditioner specifically for color-treated hair.

Where are your products made?

All CrownKinetics products are formulated and manufactured in our facility in Brooklyn, New York. Our raw ingredients are ethically sourced from women-led cooperatives in Ghana, Senegal, Jamaica, and Nigeria. Every batch undergoes third-party safety and efficacy testing before it reaches your crown.

What is your shipping policy?

We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50 within the United States (3–7 business days). Expedited shipping (1–3 business days) is available for a flat fee. We also ship internationally to select countries (7–21 business days). Visit our Shipping Policy page for full details.

Can I return a product if it does not work for me?

Absolutely. We stand behind every product with our 30-Day Crown Confidence Guarantee. If you are not fully satisfied, you can return any product within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange, even if it has been opened or partially used. See our Refund Policy for the complete process.

Are your products tested on animals?

Never. CrownKinetics is proudly cruelty-free and 100% vegan. We do not test on animals at any stage of product development, and we never use animal-derived ingredients. We are Leaping Bunny certified and committed to ethical practices throughout our entire supply chain.

Do you offer wholesale or salon partnerships?

Yes. We partner with salons, barbershops, and retailers who share our commitment to textured hair excellence. Our wholesale program includes competitive pricing, marketing support, and product training for your team. Select "Wholesale Inquiry" in the contact form above, or email us directly at wholesale@crownkinetics.com.

How should I store CrownKinetics products?

Because we use natural preservatives rather than synthetic chemicals, we recommend storing your products in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep lids tightly closed after each use. Most products have a shelf life of 12–18 months once opened. Each product label includes a Period After Opening (PAO) symbol for reference.
