## Content Guidelines

### Voice and Tone
- **Authoritative but approachable** — expert knowledge delivered like advice from a trusted friend
- **Inclusive** — acknowledge all textures, genders, ages, and stages of the hair journey
- **Empowering** — never shame product choices, past decisions (like relaxing), or hair types
- **Culturally aware** — honor the cultural significance of hair without appropriating or reducing it

### SEO Standards for Every Post
- Target keyword in title, URL slug, meta description, H1, and first 100 words
- Secondary keywords woven naturally into H2s and body content
- Internal links: minimum 3 per post (to products, collections, and related blog posts)
- External links: 1-2 per post to authoritative sources (studies, dermatologist quotes)
- Images: minimum 4 per post, all with descriptive alt text containing target keywords
- Minimum 1 CrownKinetics product mention with link (organic, not forced)
- Meta description under 160 characters, compelling and click-worthy
- URL slug: short, keyword-rich, no dates

### Promotion Strategy per Post
1. Email newsletter feature (day of publish)
2. Instagram carousel summarizing key points (day of publish)
3. Pinterest pin with custom graphic (day of publish)
4. Instagram Story with swipe-up to post (day 2)
5. TikTok educational clip derived from post content (within 1 week)
6. Re-share on social media (30 days after publish)
7. Internal linking from future posts as they publish
