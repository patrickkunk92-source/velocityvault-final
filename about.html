About VelocityVault

One Inner City Kid's Dream to Share the BMX Lifestyle

Elliott's Story

Founder & Owner

Growing up in Hudson, Florida, Elliott didn't have much—but he had a dream and a beat-up BMX bike that changed everything.

As an inner city kid, Elliott watched the older riders cruise through the streets on their SE Bikes, pulling tricks and turning heads. He knew that bike represented more than just transportation—it was freedom, style, and a ticket to something bigger.

"I saved every dollar from cutting grass, washing cars, anything I could do," Elliott recalls. "When I finally got my first SE Bike, I felt unstoppable. That bike gave me confidence, community, and a passion that never faded."

Years later, with that same hustle and determination, Elliott built VelocityVault to share that feeling with riders everywhere. From beginners to street legends, VelocityVault connects you with quality BMX bikes that fuel your passion—just like that first bike did for Elliott.

"Every rider deserves to feel what I felt. VelocityVault is my way of giving back to the culture that raised me." - Elliott
