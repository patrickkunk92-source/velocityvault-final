Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a credit card to join?
No! VelocityVault membership is completely free. No credit card required for activation. We only collect payment if you choose to redeem rewards for merchandise.
How long until I get my cashback?
Cashback is credited to your VelocityVault wallet within 30 days of the confirmed purchase on Amazon. Some brands offer faster approval (7-14 days).
Can I earn on my own purchases?
Yes! As a member, every purchase you make through your personal VelocityVault links earns you 5-7% cashback, depending on your tier level.
What if a purchase doesn't track?
If a purchase doesn't track within 30 days, contact our support team with your order number. We'll investigate with the brand and manually credit your account if eligible.
How do I withdraw my earnings?
Earnings can be redeemed as: (1) Exclusive VelocityVault merchandise, (2) Donated to BMX charities, (3) Stored as credit for future purchases.
Is there a minimum payout?
You can redeem as soon as you have $10 in your account. There's no upper limit on earnings or number of transactions.
