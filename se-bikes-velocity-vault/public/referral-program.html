Referral Program FAQ

When do I get paid?

Commissions are credited within 30 days of purchase confirmation. Payouts are monthly minimum $10 balance.

How long do referral links work?

30-day cookie duration. If someone clicks your link and purchases within 30 days, you get credit (even if they buy multiple times).

Can I refer myself?

No, self-referrals are detected and excluded. You can only earn on genuine referrals from other people.

What if my referral gets a refund?

If the purchase is refunded, the commission is reversed. We only pay on net confirmed sales.

Can I run paid ads?

Yes! Facebook, Google, Instagram ads are allowed. Follow our terms of service for accurate disclosures.

Is there a limit to earnings?

No! Earn as much as you want. Top referrers are earning $5,000+ monthly.
